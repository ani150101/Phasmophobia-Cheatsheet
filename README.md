## ✨ Features

The overlay is built to float seamlessly over your game as a transparent, frameless window, giving you instant access to advanced tracking tools without losing focus on the hunt.

* 📌 **Always-On-Top & Frameless:** Blends into your gameplay. Drag to position it anywhere.
* ⌨️ **Custom Global Hotkeys:** Instantly show or hide the overlay using a customizable hotkey (Defaults to `Alt + X`).
* ⏱️ **Dynamic Smudge Timer:** Tracks your 60s (Demon), 90s (Normal), 120s (Obambo), and 180s (Spirit) safety windows with visual/audio cues.
* 👣 **Rhythm Speed Finder:** Tap to the beat of the ghost's footsteps. Features snap-to-grid accuracy, a 12-tap shock-absorbing buffer, and an inline history log.
* 🎵 **Ghost Metronome:** Audio references for every exact ghost speed in the game to train your ear.
* 🩸 **Modifier Support:** Toggle "Blood Moon" (+15% speed) or custom game speed multipliers, and the Speed Finder math updates instantly.
* 📖 **Zero-Evidence Survival Guide:** Flip any ghost card to reveal specialized behavior tests and looping strategies.

## 🚀 Installation (Portable)

This app is entirely self-contained. No installers, no bloating your registry, and no Node.js required on your end.

1. Navigate to the **[Releases](../../releases/latest)** tab on the right side of this repository.
2. Download the `PhasmoOverlay-Standalone-v1.0.0.exe` file.
3. Drop the `.exe` anywhere on your PC (like your Desktop or Gaming folder).
4. Double-click to launch. 

> **Note:** Ensure Phasmophobia is set to **Borderless Windowed** in your graphics settings. Exclusive Fullscreen blocks third-party overlays from drawing on top of the game.

## 🕹️ Usage

| Control | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| **`Alt + X`** | Show / Hide the overlay (Default). |
| **Drag Header** | Click and hold the top bar to move the overlay around your screen. |
| **Toggle Hotkey** | Click the hotkey button in the Settings panel and press your new key combination (e.g., `Ctrl + Shift + Z`) to rebind. |
| **Accuracy Toggle** | Change the Tap Tolerance (Strict to Forgiving) if your rhythm is slightly off during high-stress hunts. |

## 🛠️ Built With

* **HTML5 / CSS3 / Vanilla JS:** For an ultra-lightweight, lightning-fast UI.
